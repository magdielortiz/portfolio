4th San Juan
Graphic Triennial

Visual identity system for the 4th San Juan Poly/Graphic Triennial, Displaced Images / Images in Space. We worked on a graphic system based on the curatorial and poly/graphic concept, exploring techniques from traditional print making and their manifestation on contemporary graphic images. The project includes the design of a graphic mark, signage system, exhibitions graphics, promotional objects, posters, printed matter, and a catalogue.
